Calix Media – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/22/2025

Calix Media (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum personal information required to provide our services, including:

  • TikTok account information (such as username, profile information, and authentication tokens)
  • Content you choose to upload (such as videos and captions)
  • Usage statistics (such as upload status or error logs, for debugging and support)

We do not collect unnecessary personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Allow you to log in with your TikTok account and authenticate your identity
  • Upload and publish content to your TikTok account on your behalf, as you request
  • Display your account information, status, and statistics within the Calix Media dashboard
  • Provide support and improve our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties except as required to operate our automation features.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your information is only stored as needed to perform the automated upload tasks you request.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and never shared.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties, except TikTok as required to process uploads and content on your behalf.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain your data only as long as needed to provide the service and fulfill your requests.
  • Uploaded videos and temporary data may be deleted after uploads are complete.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your information by contacting us at richardcatalan1410@gmail.com. You may revoke access to your TikTok account at any time through the TikTok platform or your account settings.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Your continued use of Calix Media after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
